# Library Policies

Welcome to our library! We're happy to help you find your next great read.

## Borrowing Rules
- **Limit**: Members can borrow up to **5 books** at a time.
- **Duration**: Books can be kept for **14 days**.
- **Renewals**: You can renew a book once for an additional 7 days if no one else has reserved it.

## Membership
- Membership is free for all city residents.
- To sign up, please bring a valid ID and proof of address.

## Late Fees
- Late books incur a fee of **$0.50 per day**.
- If a book is lost, the member is responsible for the full replacement cost plus a $5 processing fee.

## Quiet Zone
- The second floor is a dedicated quiet zone. Please keep conversations to a minimum.
- No food is allowed in the library; drinks in sealed containers are permitted.
